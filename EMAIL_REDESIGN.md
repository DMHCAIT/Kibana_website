## 📊 Design Specifications

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: `#1a1a1a` (Deep Black - Premium)
- **Secondary**: `#2d2d2d` (Dark Gray)
- **Accent**: `#b0b0b0` (Light Gray text)
- **Highlight**: `#b8860b` (Gold - Security section)
- **Background**: `#f5f5f5` to `#ffffff` (Soft gradient)

### **Typography**
- **Font Family**: System fonts (Apple, Segoe UI, Roboto)
- **Headings**: 32px, Bold, Letter-spaced
- **Body**: 14px, Regular
- **OTP Code**: 48px, Monospace, Bold
- **Labels**: 12px, Uppercase, Letter-spaced

### **Spacing & Layout**
- **Max Width**: 600px (optimal email width)
- **Padding**: Generous (40px sections)
- **Border Radius**: 12px (modern rounded corners)
- **Shadows**: Subtle for depth
